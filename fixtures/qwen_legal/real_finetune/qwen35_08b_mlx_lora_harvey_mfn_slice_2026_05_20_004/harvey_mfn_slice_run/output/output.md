2. **Recommendation:**

   - The LP challenge risk persists despite the fact that Whitmore is below the MFN threshold.

4. **Action Required:**
   - The GP should proceed with the reclassification of Great Plains as MFN-eligible.
   - All other parties should be treated as eligible.
